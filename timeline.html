Timeline

Moon 70-76

  • Wolfstep flees with Ravenkit, the pair making it for 6 moons together. On the night of Ravenkit’s apprentice ceremony, Wolfstep dies. Ravenkit vows to always keep her clan name and sets off to find her brother. (Moon 76)
  • Redheart opts to save Shadowkit rather than Kestrelfall, Silverstorm torn apart that her mother dies. Silverstorm attempts to kill Shadowkit but Redstar fights back, scarring Silverstorm’s face and saving Shadowkit. Shadowkit’s back left leg is permanently twisted and scarred. (Moon 70)
  • RushClan is destroyed, with Rowanstar dying. Kestrelfall escapes with Redheart, Shadowkit, and Silverstorm, making it to the tunnels outside of RushClan. While traversing these tunnels, they collapse.

Moon 60-70

  • Ravenkit and Shadowkit are born to Kestrelfall and Wolfstep. (Moon 70)
  • Twolegs are noticed at the edge of the territories, and RushClan watches in horror as both MarshClan and YewClan are destroyed. As RushClan lives deeper in the forest away from human territory, RushClan believes they may survive.
  • Wolfstep talks to Redheart about the prophecy she is in, and promises her that she will become deputy when Rowanstar passes and Wolfstep takes leadership. It is her destiny to lead RushClan.
  • Flamepaw and Silverpaw receive their warrior names. Silverpaw becomes Silverstorm, but Flamepaw wants to return to the name Ivy gave her. She becomes Redstrike, but Kestrelfall renames her to Redheart, to honor her loyalty. (Moon 68)

Moon 50-60

  • Wolfstep and Kestrelfall agree to have kits.
  • Rowanstar tries stepping down from leadership as more and more cats call him “Rowanwhisker.” Wolfstep tries to support his friend.
  • During training practice, Silverpaw tears Flamepaw’s ear off, leaving her permanently scarred. Kestrelfall praises the strong will and fierce attacks as the makings of a warrior. (Moon 60).
  • Flamekit and Silverkit become Flamepaw and Silverpaw, Flamepaw training under Kestrelfall and Silverpaw training under Jaystrike. (Moon 55)

Moon 48-50

  • Flamekit is viewed as the matron of fire.
  • Kestrelfall adopts Storm and changes her name to Silverkit. Red is taken in by Lynxfire, her name being changed (by Kestrelfall) to Flamekit.
  • Ivy realizes she cannot provide for two kits and gives them to Kestelfall to take to the clans. (Moon 50)
  • Ivy cannot provide for Red, and one day wakes up to a newborn kit sitting next to Red. Ivy names her Storm.
  • Ivy gives birth to Redbud, Cyprus, and Willow. Redbud lives. Kestrelfall helps with the kitting. Redbud’s name is changed to Red. (Moon 48)

Moon 30-45

  • Kestrelfall finds Ivy wondering the territories, pregnant (Moon 45)
  • Wolfstep begins being called “Wolfstar”, as he leads more often than Rowanstar who is shocked by the violence of the clans and the anguish of leadership. This divides the clan. (Moon 32)
  • Lynxfire steps down from deputyship, preferring a warrior’s life. Wolfstep appointed deputy (Moon 32)
  • Ivy is brought to the clans and rejected by Reedfeather and RushClan. Snowtuft leaves.
  • Ivy is born to Snowtuft and Sweetcream. (Moon 30)

Moon 15-25

  • Yewfang dies in her sleep. Wolfstep and Kestrelfall wake up to a sun and moon emblazoned on them, marking them as the next guardians.
  • Sedgestar dies during border skirmish. Rowanwhisker becomes leader and appoints Lynxfire deputy. (Moon 24)
  • Dawnfur attacks Talonheart and receives a scar. Talonheart exiled. Nightpaw receives his full medicine cat name, Nightstorm. Rowanwhisker is appointed deputy (Moon 23)
  • Nightpaw reveals deputy Talonheart’s betrayal, as he tries to wrest control of the clans.

Moon 0-12

  • Partridgepool dies of Greencough, Talonheart appointed as new deputy. (Moon 12)
  • Nightpaw is apprenticed to Dawnfur, becoming the newest Medicine Cat Apprentice (Moon 7)
  • Flamekit is a red, tortie chimera male, destroying the hope of being a kit of prophecy.
  • Kestrelkit, Shrewkit, Flamekit, and Nightkit are born to Talonheart and Emberfur. (Moon 1)

The Distant Past

  • Prophecy is given, regarding a “Matron of Fire whose flames will ignite the clans.”
  • Moon Guardian Hoptail retires, marking Yewkit as future Moon Guardian.
  • YewClan leader Sorrelstar dies, becoming the sun guardian.
  • MarshClan forms with Pinestar as leader, and Ridgefoot as deputy.
  • RushClan introduces the Moonsleep.
  • Sedgestar ascends to leadership, appointing Partridgepool as deputy.
